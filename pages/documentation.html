Canon EOS RP Setup Guide

Package Contents:

  • EOS RP Camera Body
  • Battery Pack LP-E17
  • Battery Charger LC-E17
  • Camera Cover R-F-5
  • Wide Neck Strap
  • RF24-105mm F4-7.1 IS STM Lens
  • Lens Cap E-67II
  • Lens Dust Cap RF

Attaching the Lens:

  1. Remove the lens cap from the lens.
  2. Align the white or red dot on the lens with the corresponding dot on the camera's lens mount.
  3. Rotate the lens clockwise until it clicks into place.

Capturing Photos:

  1. Set the mode dial to "P" (Program AE) for automatic shooting.
  2. Press the shutter button halfway to autofocus.
  3. Press the shutter button fully to take a photo.

Powering On/Off:

1. To power on: Set the power switch to the ON position.

2. To power off: Set the power switch to the OFF position when not in use.

Inserting the Memory Card:

  1. Insert a compatible SD memory card into the memory card slot.
  2. Ensure the card's label faces the back of the camera.
  3. Push the card until it clicks into place.
