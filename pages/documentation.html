Canon EOS RP Documentation

Package Contents:

  • EOS RP Camera Body
  • Battery Pack LP-E17
  • Battery Charger LC-E17
  • Camera Cover R-F-5
  • Wide Neck Strap
  • RF24-105mm F4-7.1 IS STM Lens
  • Lens Cap E-67II
  • Lens Dust Cap RF

Powering On/Off:

  • 1. To power on: Set the power switch to the ON position.
  • 2. To power off: Set the power switch to the OFF position when not in use.

Inserting the Memory Card:

  • Insert a compatible SD memory card into the memory card slot.
  • Ensure the card's label faces the back of the camera.
  • Push the card until it clicks into place.

Attaching the Lens:

  • Remove the lens cap from the lens.
  • Align the white or red dot on the lens with the corresponding dot on the camera's lens mount.
  • Rotate the lens clockwise until it clicks into place.

Capturing Photos:

  • Set the mode dial to "P" (Program AE) for automatic shooting.
  • Press the shutter button halfway to autofocus.
  • Press the shutter button fully to take a photo.
